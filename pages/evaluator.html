What is an Improof Teacher Partner?

Improof connects people that want certifications with intitutions or evaluators that can provide them. If you are a a certified teacher or representative from a college or a university we invite you to join us.

What will I do?

As an evaluator, you can create your own certification and earn profit from students taking it.

What will Improof do?

Improof will give you all te virtual tools you need to ensure a good quality certifications and asses your certification so it is transparent, and therefore valid.

How should I start?

The first step is to fill the forms below, including the main documentation that proves your are qualified to be an examinator. This includes your degrees and documentation that proves you are currently working at an institution as a teacher or evaluator. Improof will revise the information you sent and will contact you for further questions aor to welcome you to our virtual campus.
